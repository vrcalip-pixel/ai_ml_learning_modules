Ethical Considerations

Healthcare and public services AI raise unique ethical challenges:

‚ö†Ô∏è Privacy Concerns

Issue: Medical records are extremely sensitive. AI requires vast patient data.

Risks:

  • Data breaches expose private health information
  • Insurance companies could use AI predictions to deny coverage
  • Employers might discriminate based on health predictions

Safeguards: HIPAA regulations, data anonymization, strict access controls, encryption

‚öñÔ∏è Bias & Fairness

Issue: AI trained on non-diverse data can be biased.

Real Example: Skin cancer AI trained mostly on light skin ‚Üí less accurate for darker skin tones, leading to misdiagnosis.

Another Example: Pulse oximeters (measure blood oxygen) less accurate on darker skin, worsened COVID outcomes for Black patients.

Solutions: Train AI on diverse patient populations, test for bias across demographics, include diverse teams in AI development

üîç Transparency & Explainability

Issue: "Black box" AI makes life-or-death decisions doctors don't fully understand.

Scenario: AI recommends aggressive cancer treatment. Doctor asks "Why?" AI can't explain its reasoning.

Why it matters: Doctors need to understand recommendations to trust them and explain to patients.

Solutions: Explainable AI (XAI) that shows reasoning, human-in-the-loop decision making, AI as assistant not replacement

üåç Equity & Access

Issue: Will AI worsen healthcare disparities?

Concerns:

  • Wealthy hospitals get AI, rural/poor hospitals don't
  • AI requires internet/smartphones‚Äîexcluding low-income patients
  • Language barriers (AI trained on English)

Solutions: Subsidize AI for underserved hospitals, develop low-tech alternatives, multilingual AI, telemedicine to extend access

üéì Ethical Framework for Healthcare/Public Service AI

  • Do no harm: Patient safety first, rigorous testing
  • Informed consent: Patients know when AI is used
  • Human oversight: AI assists, humans decide
  • Fairness: Test for bias, ensure equity
  • Privacy: Protect sensitive data
  • Transparency: Explain AI decisions
